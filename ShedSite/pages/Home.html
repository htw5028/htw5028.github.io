Personal training and community

The Shed is not only a place to recieve personal training for anyone of any level, it has also become its own community. There is something for everyone no matter the goal or the circumstance you can find what you are looking for. We offer hands on and off training, and programs for weightloss, strength, and more. Also feel free to share your milestones to celebrate with our community and allow everyone to follow your progress and support you.       

Owner

Steven Izzi is the Owner and creater of The Shed LLC. The Shed was created by Steven during Covid when gyms were shut down to allow a place for him and his friends to work out and has florished from there. As he progressed with his studies and training he took on clients for personal training.
